Ferrero Collection

Ferrero Collection includes the following varieties: Ferrero Rondnoir, Ferrero Rocher® and Raffaello®. Rocher® chocolates are a tempting combination of luscious, creamy, chocolaty filling surrounding a whole hazelnut, within a delicate crisp wafer…all enveloped in milk chocolate and finely chopped hazelnuts. Raffaello® confections are a harmonious blend of carefully selected ingredients, including white Californian almonds and coconut from the Pacific Islands. Rondnoir® fine dark chocolates provide an intense taste of dark chocolate, with a crisp wafer surrounding a creamy, chocolaty filling with a dark chocolate pearl at the center. A delicate and refined pleasure.

M&M's Variety

M&M's are multi-colored button-shaped chocolates, each of which has the letter "m" printed in lower case in white on one side, consisting of a candy shell surrounding a filling which varies depending upon the variety of M&M's.
